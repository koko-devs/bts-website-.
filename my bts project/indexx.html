BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a South Korean boy group that debuted on June 13, 2013, under BigHit
Entertainment.The group consists of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.With their unique
music style, powerful performances, and inspiring messages about self-love, hope, and growth, BTS has
become one of the most successful and influential music groups in the world. In the following sections, we’ll
explore why BTS is so popular among teenagers and introduce each member of the group in detail

Kim Namjoon, known by his stage name RM, is the leader and one of the main members of BTS. He was born on September 12, 1994 in South Korea and is currently 31 years old. His thoughtful, calm, and wise leadership is considered one of the key reasons for BTS’s cohesion and global success. RM is a rapper, songwriter, and music producer, and he has written or directly contributed to a large portion of BTS’s songs. His lyrics are often deep, emotional, and filled with philosophical messages about hope, self-awareness, and personal growth. He has a strong passion for books, art, museums, and music, reflecting his love for culture and learning in both his personal life and artistic work. Through supporting cultural projects, charitable activities, and promoting reading, RM has inspired countless young people around the world. He is not just an artist he is a cultural and intellectual leader whose music continues to touch minds and hearts globally.

Kim Seokjin, known by his stage name Jin, is the oldest member and one of the main members of BTS. He was born on December 4, 1992, and is currently 32 years old. Jin is a vocalist of the group, known for his high confidence, and is famously called Worldwide Handsome”, a title he proudly embraces. He is also known for being kind and caring toward his fans, always showing appreciation and respect for them. His bright personality, charm, and natural presence make him a beloved member both on stage and off.

Min Yoongi, known by his stage name Suga, is one of the main members of BTS. He was born on March 9
1993, in South Korea and is currently 32 years old. Suga is the main rapper of the group and is recognized
as one of thebest rappers in K-pop. His unique and professional rap style adds depth and character to
BTS’s music. He is also very popular for his humble, calm, and thoughtful personality, and he is usually
less active on social media,which makes him a bit mysterious and even more intriguing to fans.

Jung Ho-seok, known by his stage name J-Hope, is one of the main members of BTS. He was born on February 18, 1994, in South Korea and is currently 31 years old. J-Hope is the group’s rapper, vocalist, and best dancer in K-pop. He is known for his energetic performances, professional techniques, and creative dance moves, and his unique style always captures the attention of fans. His presence on stage is full of energy and excitement, turning every performance into an unforgettable experience. He is also admired for his hard work, dedication, and passion for music and dance,making him one of the most inspiring members of BTS.

Park Ji-min, known by his stage name Jimin, is one of the main members of BTS. He was born on October 13, 1995, in South Korea and is currently 30 years old. Jimin is a vocalist and dancer of the group, known for his expressive performances and graceful dance style. He is naturally shy, but on stage he shows a unique confidence and charisma, captivating fans with every performance. He is affectionately nicknamed Chick” (Jooje) by fans because of his gentle and lovable personality. The combination of tenderness, dedication, and stage presence makes Jimin one of the most beloved members of BTS.

Kim Tae-hyung, known by his stage name V, is one of the main members of BTS. He was born on December
30, 1995, in South Korea and is currently 29 years old. V is a vocalist of the group, known for his deep and
soulful voice and unique artistic style. He is admired for his charismatic stage presence, expressive
performances, and creative personality, making him stand out in both music and visual arts. He is also
recognized as one of the most handsome men in the world, adding to his global popularity. Off-stage, he has a
calm and slightly mysterious personality, which adds to his charm, and he is very loved by fans for his kindness,
humor, andgenuine interactions. V is also passionate about photography, art, and fashion, often showcasing
his creativity
beyond music.

Jeon Jung-kook, known by his stage name Jungkook, is the youngest member of BTS. He was born on September 1, 1997, in South Korea and is currently 27 years old. Jungkook is the main vocalist of the group and is known for his powerful voice, versatile skills, and strong stage presence. Despite being the youngest member, he consistently impresses fans with his talent, dedication, and professionalism. He is affectionately nicknamed “Rabbit”by BTS fans (ARMY) because of his cute and energetic personality. The combination of charm, skill, and hard work makes Jungkook one of the most beloved members of the group.
